About Us

We cater to all your rock and metal thirsts. We've got classic, progressive, punk and grunge! Power metal, thrash metal, alternative and industrial! If you want to expand your music tastes or just find some folks who like the same music you do, you can find us here!

We're a friendly society who meet every Monday in term time for socials, every evening from 8.30-11:30.

We're open minded and welcome all, whether you're a big music fan or just curious about rock, punk and metal. We have our own DJs who play your requests all night!

Official membership of the society for a year will set you back £4-6, and for this, you get money off any out-of-town trips RocSoc runs (usually two), as well as money off entry to any extra RocSoc- run events.
